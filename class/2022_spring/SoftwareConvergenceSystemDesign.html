Course Description

Students will experience the overall engineering design process from the perspective of computer engineering. This course will cover the topics of project selection, specification, concept generation, concept evaluation, Unified Modeling Language (UML), teamwork, and how to give effective oral presentations. Most of the student efforts will be made through team projects.
